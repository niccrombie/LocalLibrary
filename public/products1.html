Award Winning Razer Products

Razer is one of the leading products in esports and the triple-headed snake is possibly the most widly recognized trademark in the gaming community.

Founded in 2005 have rapidly spread around the world now having 9 offices worldwide.

Razer's award-winning hardware range from the highest of quality high-performance gaming peripherals to their always popular high end light weight gaming laptop. They have recently launched their own cell phone product with all the latest technology and features.
